About Impulsar Infotech

Impulsar Infotech is a private limited company incorporated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, specializing in business services, with a core focus on IT consulting and solutions. We are committed to helping our clients propel their business outcomes. The name "Impulsar" is a Spanish word that means "to propel," and that is the philosophy we live by.

Our team provides end-to-end services in quality assurance (QA) and project delivery, ensuring a value-based partnership that is sustained through in-depth knowledge of our customers' business problems. We have developed a robust model that includes specialized training, strategic staffing, and high-end consulting to deliver cost-efficient, long-term resolutions.
